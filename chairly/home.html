Cecily

An old soul for the ages, Cecily is a lady with a strong opinion and wears her frivolities like a badge of honor. With her unassuming blend of traditional oak and clean deco lines, this grande dame commands the attention of the room without saying a word. Oh wouldn't you like to be a fly on the wall when she's the belle of the ball?




Derek

Whoever said practical can't be beautiful obviously leads a sad life. If so, maybe Derek can coax them out of their gloom to awaken their inner idealists. Like a forever-optimistic personal trainer, Derek is always projecting his best self with his sleek, poly-resin seat and back, firmly mounted on solid pine legs and powdered steel stretchers. Now is the time to cultivate a daily habit of good design and Derek will get you there!
