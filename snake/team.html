Our Team





Squeaky McSqueakers

Chairman/CEO

Hailing from the prestigious Rhode Island School of Design, where he graduated summa cum laude with a degree in forestry with a specialization in nuts and legumes, Squeaky founded Chairly as a way to elevate a design-forward way of thinking. He, and Chairly, got off the ground in late 2016 and has continually risen in the domain of home design, putting Squeaky on the same level with the best design minds of his generation, plus or minus five generations.





The Baroness Fifi von Ratmeyer

Designer/Sourcing

The Baroness Fifi von Ratmeyer, or, known to her friends as "The Baroness Fifi von Ratmeyer", heads Charily's design and sourcing team. An autodidact, the Baroness combines her unconventional design ideals, with a focus on sewer lines and high drama, with a nose towards comfort and the warm fuzzies. Not one to rest on her laurels, the Baroness plans to expand Chairly's offerings to include footstools later this year.





Mo

Sales/Website

Mo, the head of online sales, may be the most interesting rodent in the world. When Mo was just a babybara, he won the title of both the National Spelling Bee, and the National Spelling Aey. Though he lacks fingers, Mo bowls a 300. He is the current world poker champion, using only animal crackers. Chairly is extrememly lucky to have Mo. Though he rarely sits in chairs, when he does, it's a Chairly chair.
